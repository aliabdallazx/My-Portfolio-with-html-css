My Journey in Business & Technology

From a young age, I've been fascinated by the intersection of business and technology. Growing up in an entrepreneurial family, I started my first small business at just 12 years old, learning valuable lessons about customer needs, market dynamics, and strategic thinking.

This early exposure to business fundamentals combined with my passion for technology led me to pursue a career where I could bridge the gap between strategic business thinking and technical execution.

Today, I specialize in creating user-centered digital solutions that not only look great but also drive measurable business results. My unique background allows me to understand both the technical requirements and the strategic objectives of every project.
